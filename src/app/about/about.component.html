About Us

Student Management System is a web-based application designed to help educational institutions manage students, teachers, courses, attendance, and academic records efficiently.

Features

Student Management

Add, update, view and manage student information easily

Teacher Management

Manage teacher details and their subject assignments

Course Management

Organize and manage courses and academic subjects

Attendance Tracking

Track and monitor student attendance efficiently

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify academic administration through a secure, efficient and user-friendly system that helps educational institutions focus more on education and less on paperwork.
